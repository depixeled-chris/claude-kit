(decision, R052) SMOOTH TERRAIN FIELD, but KEEP TERRACING (they are different things). Marble-madness quantized the TERRAIN ITSELF into discrete height bands by default — we do NOT copy that: HOD's raw terrain field (terrain_height) is a CONTINUOUS SMOOTH heightfield (fBm), rolling hills, no band-stepping. BUT we STILL TERRACE: terracing is the CONSTRUCTED cut/fill flat PADS — block/lot/parking pads are flat terraces carved into / built onto the smooth hills, with retaining walls at the edges (the Pad model: pad_y a chosen flat level, snap_to_terrace KEPT so adjacent blocks step between terrace levels = the SimCity terraced-city-on-smooth-hills look). So: change = remove quantize() from the raw terrain field only; KEEP Pad + snap_to_terrace (the pad terracing). Update R052 §2/§3.1 to reflect smooth-field + constructed-terraces. Project: HOD.
