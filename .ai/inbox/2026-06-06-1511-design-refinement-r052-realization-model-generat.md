(design/refinement, R052) REALIZATION MODEL: generate-then-flatten/smooth. Rather than raw terrain + separately-placed pad geometry, the terrain layer exposes ONE effective height_at(seed,x,z): start from the base quantized terrain, then FLATTEN it under parcel/block/parking/park SLABS (to pad_y) and SMOOTH/GRADE it along ROAD corridors (bounded-slope longitudinal profile). The mesh, collision, camera, spawn all read this one graded heightfield. Cut/fill (retaining, plinth, embankment) is simply where a flattened/graded region MEETS the surrounding raw terrain (a step/skirt). Flatten-under-rect (pad_y = fn of terrain under the rect) and smooth-along-corridor (bounded-slope fit along the centreline) are PURE deterministic ops — no iterative neighbour state — so determinism/seam-stability hold. This simplifies R052 §3.3-3.7: the Pad/grade is data describing the flatten; the heightfield modification realizes it; one height_at is the seam. P0 mesh = raw field (flat-city-rides-it); P1/P2 flatten under slabs; P4 smooth/grade roads. Project: HOD.
